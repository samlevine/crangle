crangle by samlevine

iPhone app that lets your friends know when you'll be there!

(Eventually) Email your location, speed and ETA to friends. They don't need Crangle to read the e-mail, just eyballs, text to speech, etc. Your friends can use crangle to reply back with their location, speed and ETA. Features: - transportation method: walking, bike, car (friends don't let friends crangle while driving).
